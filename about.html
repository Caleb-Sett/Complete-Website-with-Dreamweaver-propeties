Meet this Great Company!!

Wazishare Solutions Limited Company is a Company registered as per the companyâ€™s act of and is mandated to undertake varied businesses. Currently we are in the telecommunication space operating as a Content Service Provider offering convenient and cheap alternative mobile payment application that support submission of Health insurance premiums by the self-employed Kenyans. The users will use mobile phone airtime to make the monthly premiums.

The application is known as AIRTIME SERVICE which is a product of Wazishare Solutions. In relation to the use of the system, the company has acquired all the relevant documentation including licensing from Communication Authority.

The development of this application was preceded by a lot of research work on health care financing which revealed that Cover is the most popular with a popularity index of almost 80% attracted to the facility. However, there are many citizens who cannot consistently pay for insurance Cover making them unable to access medical cover should they be in need. Some of them have had to consume all their financial assets to acquire medication due to lack of medical cover. This application is therefore coming as a blessing to such individuals because they will be able to accumulate their contributions towards insurance Cover in the most convenient way possible; using airtime through Lipa Na Airtime application. We also belief that we are playing a wider role in the implementation of the government big four agenda on the provision of health care.

The company has carried out registration of new users across the country since 2018 and it is planning a mass registration in 2019 and a complete deployment of the system so that users can fully consume the service of Airtime.The Company offices are located in acassia Court Block C4 Kiambere Road Upper Hill,Which acts as the focal point for all business operations. The operational work is mainly field work involving recruitment of new users across the country.
