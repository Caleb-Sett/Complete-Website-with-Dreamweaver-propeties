Possible Accounts

Wazishare solutions company limited is offering this service whose benefits to the clients far outweigh the cost they will incur to use the service. Below is a summary of value proposition to the end user based on the nature of the system.

INDIVIDUAL ACCOUNT:
This is an account where a user contributes alone to make up for the minimum amount required.
HOUSEHOLD ACCOUNT:
The account creates a provision for family members to pool resources towards one account; husband and wife or children can share the daily target so that instead of one person paying sh.25, the value will reduce proportionately
MEDISHARE ACCOUNT:
we have also provided a unique feature where every other user can accumulate resources in the system which will then be converted into Medishare points which are transferable to others whenever requested.
COMPASSIONATE ACCOUNT:
This is a resource mobilization platform aimed at supporting vulnerable members of the community. If a person wishes to pay for another person he or she will use this account.
